SANTOS DUMONT

The father of the aviation

Accomplishments

  • 1898 - Designed and flew his first personal balloon named "Bresil".
  • 1898 to 1905 - Designed, built and flew 11 dirigibles. Santos-Dumont used them to fly around Paris.
  • 1905 - Completed the design of his first fixed wing aircraft and a helicopter.
  • 1906 - Took a flight in the 14-Bis, of 220 meters in 21.5 secods on November 12 1906. This flight marked the first world record recognized by the Federation Aeronautique Internationale.
  • 1907 to 1908 - The Demoiselle monoplane was created and commercialized.
  • 1910 - The magazine Popular Mechanics published the drawings of the Demoiselle and wrote "This machine is better than any other which has ever been built ..."
